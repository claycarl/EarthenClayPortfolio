About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m a filmmaker and visual storyteller working primarily in documentary and place-based work. While my projects span both human and environmental subjects, my focus is always on story and on creating work that feels grounded. My process is intentional and observational. I spend time listening and learning context to help guide the my storytelling process. I’m interested in moments that unfold naturally rather than those that are overly directed, and I try to work in ways that are respectful of both the subject and the environment I’m filming in. Visually, my work favors restraint and texture over spectacle. I’m drawn to natural light, subtle pacing, and the quiet details that give a story its emotional weight. Place plays an important role in my work, not as a backdrop but as something that actively shapes the people, histories, and experiences within the frame. I often collaborate with brands, artists, and other filmmakers, helping translate complex or overlooked subjects into clear, human stories without simplifying them. Across all projects, my goal is to create stories that are rooted in real experience and meant to last beyond the moment they’re seen.
